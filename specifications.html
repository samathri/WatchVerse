Technical Specifications
The TAG Heuer Connected Calibre E4 Golf Edition features a 45mm titanium case with a matte sandblasted DLC coating, providing both durability and a sleek, lightweight feel.

The watch comes with a premium rubber strap that is both comfortable and functional, designed to fit wrist sizes ranging from 140mm to 220mm. The strap is secured with a stainless steel deployant clasp for a secure and adjustable fit, making it suitable for various activities.

Powered by a rechargeable lithium-ion battery, the watch offers up to 24 hours of usage on a full charge. It charges quickly through a magnetic dock, making it convenient for daily use without long charging times.

The TAG Heuer Connected Calibre E4 Golf Edition is equipped with a 1.4-inch OLED touchscreen display. It has a resolution of 400 x 400 pixels, providing sharp visuals and vibrant colors. The display is always-on and customizable, allowing users to choose from a variety of watch faces.
